### Project Description
It allows one to set a monthly budget and savings goals. Helps you to track your expenditures and allows you to identify on what you have spend the most. Similarly allows you to track your savings and add to income. There is also a calender allowing you to track how much you have spend on each day.

### The Problem statement
Many students and young adults struggle to manage their finances due to lack of awarness of their their budget, spending habits, and savings goals.

### The Solution
Pocketwise addresses this problem by providing a simple, visual, and interactice platform to track expenses, monitor remaining balance, and achieve saving goals effectively.
